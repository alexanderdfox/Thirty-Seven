How to Play

  • You are the camera. Forward = where you look.
  • Mouse: Look. Click to lock.
  • Shift / Ctrl: Move into / out of the scene
  • W / S: Move up / down (view space)
  • A / D: Move left / right
  • Q / E: Roll
  • V: Jump burst (view up)
  • R: Boost (view direction + invuln)
  • Get near souls (grey spheres) to awaken them
  • Awakened souls turn amber—reach the goal to advance
  • Avoid the Pale Ones (pale spheres)—they hunt you
  • SPACE: BREATHE, then LIGHTS OUT to become invulnerable
  • During fall/rise you cannot be caught. ESC to unlock mouse.
